POLYGON

Using Mesh related tools to form polygons on your object in the Deformer log.

It has the form of multiple triangles where every triangle controls the shape of the area of mesh within. Move the top on the polygon to deform the art meshl.

Noted:
Mesh tool is on the tool list on the top of the main screen.
Polygons have to be in groups of three, or it might be deformed or broken in future processes.

DEFORMER

Deformers can deform multiple selected objects. To do so, one has to select all the objects you want to put in a group, and click the warp deformer tool on the top.

Different from polygons, deformers are made of multiple squares along with multiple levers to make smaller adjustments.

Noted:
A Deformer can also contain other deformers.

ROTATION

Rotation deformer is similar to skeleton in many other animation softwares where one can use it to make objects which are contained by rotation deformers rotate according to certain set axis.

Other than that, it functions just like a warp deformer, sharing all its special features. It tool placement is also right next to the warp deformer.

Noted:
A rotation deformer containing other rotation deformers can effectively mimic the rotation of a multi-joint body part.

PATH

Deformer path is the first tool in the rightest trio of tools. When you tap it on a selected object, it creates a single red dot. Red dots will connect with each other to form a path.

When moving these dots, they will pin each other to express natural feedback. Such a tool is perfect for deforming objects without obvious joints like hair and stream of liquid.

Noted:
This tool has little effect on objects of larger scale.

PARAMETER

This tool is listed right next to one’s materials, each bar or parameter can record a certain movement with the little dots on the bar.

Users may register any object or deformer to the parameter. Setting various poses on the dots, and the software will calculate the transition between dots for the user.

Noted:
objects have to be registered to parameters or its change will not be registered.
an object or deformer cannot be registered to more than two parameters.
